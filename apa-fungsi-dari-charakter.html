7. carilah cerita naratif bebas bisa di perpustakaan atau di buka goegle setelah dibaca dpahami ditulis 1. menganalisa charakter nya 2. Menulis permasalahan nya 3. Menulis the problem resolved nya mohon bantuannya ya kak besok dikumpulkan​


Once upon a time, there was a young man named Jack. He was a hardworking and ambitious person, but he had a tendency to be arrogant and dismissive of others' opinions. He believed that his way was the only right way to do things.

One day, Jack was given a project to lead at work. He was excited to take on the challenge and immediately began delegating tasks to his team members. However, his teammates were not pleased with his dismissive attitude and began to lose motivation. They felt unvalued and unimportant, and their work began to suffer.

As the project progressed, Jack began to realize that his team members were not simply there to follow his orders. They had valuable skills and ideas that could contribute to the success of the project. He also realized that his arrogance was causing problems not just at work, but also in his personal relationships.

Determined to change his ways, Jack apologized to his team and began to actively seek out their input and ideas. He also made an effort to be more humble and listen to others' perspectives. His team members noticed the change in him and began to work together more effectively. The project was completed successfully, and Jack received recognition for his leadership.

From that experience, Jack learned that being humble and valuing others' contributions can lead to greater success, not just in his career, but also in his personal life. He made a point to continue to work on being a better listener and collaborator, which improved his relationships with others and brought him more fulfillment.

1. Jack is a hardworking and ambitious person who tends to be arrogant and dismissive of others.

2. The problem is that Jack's attitude is causing problems at work and in his personal relationships.

3. The problem is resolved when Jack learns to be humble, listen to others and value their contributions, which leads to greater success in his career and personal life

Novel

* Judul: The Lord of the Rings

* Tema: Fantasi

* Karakter/Pemain: Frodo Baggins, Samwise Gamgee, Merry, Pippin, Aragorn, Legolas, Gimli, Gandalf, Saruman, Sauron

* Plot: Frodo Baggins, seorang hobbit, ditugaskan untuk menghancurkan Cincin Utama, sebuah artefak berbahaya yang diciptakan oleh Sauron. Frodo dan teman-temannya melakukan perjalanan ke Mordor, tempat Cincin Utama dibuat, untuk menghancurkannya.

* Setting: Dunia fantasi Middle-earth

* Sinopsis: The Lord of the Rings adalah novel fantasi karya J. R. R. Tolkien yang pertama kali diterbitkan pada tahun 1954 dan 1955. Novel ini merupakan salah satu novel terlaris sepanjang masa dan telah diadaptasi menjadi film, permainan video, dan lainnya.

Drama

* Judul: Romeo dan Juliet

* Tema: Cinta dan tragedi

* Karakter/Pemain: Romeo Montague, Juliet Capulet, Mercutio, Benvolio, Tybalt, Lady Capulet, Capulet, Friar Laurence, Paris, Nurse

* Plot: Romeo dan Juliet adalah dua orang muda yang jatuh cinta, tetapi mereka berasal dari keluarga yang bermusuhan. Romeo adalah anggota keluarga Montague, sedangkan Juliet adalah anggota keluarga Capulet. Perbedaan keluarga ini membuat Romeo dan Juliet tidak dapat bersama. Mereka akhirnya memutuskan untuk menikah secara rahasia, tetapi pernikahan mereka tidak bertahan lama. Tybalt, sepupu Juliet, membunuh Mercutio, teman Romeo. Romeo yang marah membunuh Tybalt sebagai balas dendam. Romeo kemudian dibuang dari Verona. Juliet yang patah hati meminum ramuan yang membuat dia terlihat seperti mati. Romeo yang tidak tahu bahwa Juliet masih hidup, kembali ke Verona dan membunuh diri dengan meminum racun. Juliet yang terbangun dan melihat Romeo telah mati, juga membunuh diri dengan menusuk dirinya dengan pedang Romeo. Kematian Romeo dan Juliet akhirnya menyatukan kedua keluarga yang bermusuhan.

* Setting: Verona, Italia

* Sinopsis: Romeo dan Juliet adalah drama karya William Shakespeare yang pertama kali diterbitkan pada tahun 1597. Drama ini merupakan salah satu drama paling terkenal dan populer sepanjang masa.

25. apa itu NaN charakter ? gimana cara melewati nya???


NaN, singkatan dari Not a Number, adalah salah satu output dari data di ECMAscript dan turunanna (i.e. JavaScript) yang keluar kalau kita melakukan operasi yang menghasilkan output non angka (i.e. ngebagi 1 dengan 0, ngebagi angka dengan string, modulus ke string)

Cara mengatasi NaN:
1. IsNaN
2. Pastikan tidak ada operasi pembagian dengan 0
3. Pastikan tipe data yang salah diperbaiki
