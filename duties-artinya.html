13. The girls are doing their duties in that room di ubah menjadi passive


Jawaban:

Passive: Their duties are being done by the girls.

Artinya adalah tugas-tugas mereka sedang dikerjakan oleh para gadis.

Penjelasan:

Untuk mengubah kalimat aktif menjadi kalimat pasif, kita harus mengenali jenis tense yang digunakan terlebih dahulu.

Kalimat di atas menggunakan present continuous tense.

Rumus passive present continuous tense adalah

Object + am/is/are + being + verb 3 + by subject.

Active: The girls (subject) are doing (present continuous verb) their duties (object).

Passive: Their duties (object ) are being done (verb 3) by the girls (by subject).

20. Please write an essay about librarian work duties in one or two paragraph. Use the expressions that you have learned and know about the duties of librarian.​


Librarians are essential professionals who perform a wide range of duties in effectively managing libraries. They are responsible for acquiring, cataloging, and organizing library materials, ensuring that the collection remains diverse, up-to-date, and easily accessible to library users. Through their expertise in classification systems and database management, librarians facilitate the efficient retrieval of information, both physically and digitally. They provide valuable reference and research assistance, guiding patrons in their quest for knowledge and supporting them in their academic, professional, and personal pursuits.

In summary, librarians undertake a diverse range of duties to ensure the smooth functioning of libraries and to meet the informational needs of users. From managing the collection and facilitating access to resources to providing reference assistance and conducting instructional activities, librarians are indispensable in creating and maintaining an environment that fosters intellectual growth and lifelong learning.

5 DUTIES OF CUSTOMER SERVICE

Help Customers (bantu pelanggan): Customer service reps assist customers by answering questions and solving problems.

Explain Stuff (jelaskan hal): They explain products or services in a clear way.

Handle Complaints (Menangani Keluhan): They deal with customer complaints and find solutions.

Take Orders: Sometimes, they take orders and process payments.

Offer Post-Sales Help (tawarkan bantuan pasca penjualan): They provide support after a sale, like handling returns.

9 IMPORTANT PARTS OF AN APPLICATION LETTER

Your Address (alamat): Put your address and contact info at the top.

Date (tanggal): Write the date when you're writing the letter.

Company's Address (alamat perusahaan): Include the company's address you're applying to.

Opening Greeting (salam pembuka): Start with a polite greeting, like "Dear."

Intro Paragraph (Pendahuluan): In the first part, say who you are, what job you want, and how you heard about it.

Middle Paragraphs (pengembang): Use one or more paragraphs to explain why you're a good fit for the job.

Closing Paragraph (paragraf penutup): Finish by expressing your interest, asking for an interview, and saying thanks.

Closing Greeting (salam penutup): End with a polite closing, like "Sincerely."

Your Signature (tanda tangan): Sign the letter if it's on paper, or use an electronic signature if it's online.
