38. 1 pair of shoes is RMp^2 because of stock clearance sales, the store has reduced the price by 25% per pair if customers buy more than three pairs of shoes. Jasmine buys five pairs of shoes at a promotional price and pays RM633.75. Calculate the value of p. Malay/indo translate : (e) Harga sepasang kasut ialah RMp^2. Sempena jualan penghabisan stok, pihak kedai tela menurunkan harga sebanyak 25% sepasang iika pelanggan membeli lebih daripada tiga pasang kasut. Jasmine membeli lima pasang kasut dengan harga promosi dan membaya sebanyak RM633.75. Hitung nilai p.


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let’s break down the information given:

Original price per pair of shoes: RMp^2

Discount: 25% per pair

Number of pairs purchased by Jasmine: 5

Total amount paid by Jasmine: RM633.75

First, let’s calculate the discounted price per pair after the 25% reduction. The discounted price is 100% - 25% = 75% of the original price.

Discounted price per pair = 75/100 * RMp^2 = 0.75p^2

Next, we can calculate the total amount paid by Jasmine for the five pairs of shoes.

Total amount paid = Discounted price per pair * Number of pairs purchased = 5 * 0.75p^2

We are given that the total amount paid by Jasmine is RM633.75, so we can set up the equation:

5 * 0.75p^2 = RM633.75

Simplifying the equation:

0.75p^2 = RM633.75 / 5

0.75p^2 = RM126.75

Now, we can solve for the value of p by dividing both sides of the equation by 0.75 and then taking the square root:

p^2 = (RM126.75) / 0.75

p^2 = RM169

Taking the square root of both sides:

p = √(RM169)

p ≈ RM13

Therefore, the value of p is approximately RM13.
