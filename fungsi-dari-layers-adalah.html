Passive Voice

Active : Earthquake causes perpendicular layers motion of the earth.

Passive : Perpendicular layers motion of the earth is caused by earthquake.

ーーーーーーーーーーーー

Preface

Passive voice adalah salah satu grammatical voice dalam tata bahasa Inggris.

Dalam passive voice atau kalimat pasif, subjek kalimat adalah patient atau penerima aksi yang dilakukan oleh agent atau pelaku aksi.

Mari kita lihat kalimat aktif berikut:

John reads a book.

John = agent → yang melakukan aksiread = verb → aksia book = patient → penerima aksi

Kalimat pasif mempunyai konstruksi:

patient + conjugated be + past participle of verb ( + by agent)

Jadi, kalimat pasif dari John reads a book adalah:

A book is read by John.

A book = patientis = conjugated beread = past participle of the verb readJohn = agent

ーーーーーーーーーーーー

Explication

Earthquake causes perpendicular layers motion of the earth.

Earthquake = agentcauses = verbperpendicular layers motion of the earth = patient

Jadi, pasif dari kalimat tersebut adalah

Perpendicular layers motion of the earth is caused by earthquake.

Konjugasi be yang digunakan adalah is karena :

causes = verb + -s → present simple; be = am / is / aremotion = tunggal; be = is

The walls of arteries are divided into three layers, known as tunics. These layers are:

Penjelasan:

The walls of arteries are divided into three layers, known as tunics. These layers are:

1. Tunica intima: This is the innermost layer of the artery wall, consisting of a single layer of endothelial cells that line the lumen (the hollow center of the artery). The endothelial cells provide a smooth surface for blood flow and help regulate the passage of substances in and out of the artery.

2. Tunica media: This is the middle layer of the artery wall, composed of smooth muscle cells and elastic fibers. The smooth muscle cells can contract or relax to adjust the diameter of the artery and regulate blood flow. The elastic fibers provide elasticity to the artery, allowing it to expand and recoil with each heartbeat.

3. Tunica adventitia (or tunica externa): This is the outermost layer of the artery wall, consisting of connective tissue fibers. It provides support and protection to the artery.

The thickness and composition of these layers can vary depending on the type and size of the artery. Large arteries, such as the aorta, have more elastic fibers in their tunica media to handle the higher pressure of blood flow, while smaller arteries have a higher proportion of smooth muscle cells for increased contractility.
