Would prefer + to + V1 + rather than/instead of
- I would prefer to walk to your house.
I would prefer to walk to your house rather than use a train.
- I would prefer to have a dog.
I would prefer to have a dog instead of a cat.
Would prefer + noun + rather than/instead of
- I would prefer cheesecake
I would prefer cheesecake rather than strawberry cake.
- I would prefer short pants
I would prefer short pants instead of skirt.

Would rather + bare infinitive (infinitive tanpa to)/V1 + than
- I would rather be dead.
I would rather be dead than live with you.

8. Task: Make sentences by using preference1. Verb I prefer.... I like.... I would rather....2. Noun She prefers.... She likes.... She would rather... 3. Noun I prefer.... I like.... I would rather.... 4. Verb My mom prefers....My mom likes.... My mom would rather... 5. Verb I prefer... I like... I would rather...​


Jawaban:

Preference:

1. Verb

I prefer watching a movie to reading a book.

I like watching box office movies. / I like to watch box office movies.

I would rather download a movie than watch it online.

2. Noun

She prefers milk to coffee.

She likes milk. / She likes drinking milk / she likes to drink milk.

She would rather drink milk.

3. Noun

I prefer apples to oranges.

I like apples. / I like eating apples. / I like to eat apples.

I would rather eat apples than oranges.

4. Verb

My Mom prefers cooking some food to shopping.

My Mom likes cooking and cleaning at home. / My Mom likes to cook and clean at home.

My Mom would rather cook than go shopping.

5. Verb

I prefer helping others to asking for help.

I like helping others. / I like to help others.

I would rather answer some questions than ask for help.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Preference (preferensi / pilihan / kesukaan) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

(+) Subject + prefer(s) + Noun + to + Noun

Like

(+) Subject + like(s) + Verb-ing / (+) Subject + like(s) + Noun

(+) Subject + like(s) + to + Verb-1

Would rather

(+) Subject + Would rather + Verb-1 + than + Verb-1

15. fungsi I WOULD LIKE TO, I WILL, I AM GOING TO and WOULD RATHER


I would like to... = aku mau... (sopan, jadi biasanya digunakan untuk percakapan formal)
contoh:
A: "Sir, what would you like to do?"
B: "I would like to eat breakfast first."

I will... = akan (tapi ini spontan, belum direncanakan)
contoh:
A: "Your cat's gone!"
B: "What? I will look for it!"

I'm going to... = akan (tapi sudah direncakan akan ngapain sebelumnya)
contoh:
A: "Where are you going?"
B: "I'm going to the minimarket."

Would rather... = lebih baik
contoh:
A: "I'd rather stay with you than go with them."
B: "I know. I'd rather do the same thing."

40. You could go now if you want to, but you....(stay) a bit longerA. Would rather stay B. Would rather stayed C. Would rather stays D. Would rather not stay E. Would rather to stay ​


Jawaban:

A. Would rather stay

Penjelasan:

Kalimat tersebut menggunakan ungkapan "would rather", yang diikuti oleh bentuk infinitive tanpa "to".

Pilihan A, "would rather stay", adalah bentuk infinitive yang tepat untuk subjek "you".

Pilihan B, "would rather stayed", adalah bentuk past tense dan tidak cocok dengan subjek "you".

Pilihan C, "would rather stays", adalah bentuk simple present tense dan juga tidak cocok dengan subjek "you".

Pilihan D, "would rather not stay", berarti subjek "you" tidak ingin tinggal lebih lama, sehingga tidak cocok dengan konteks kalimat.

Pilihan E, "would rather to stay", salah karena tidak mengikuti bentuk infinitive tanpa "to" setelah "would rather".
