31. the sign beside means


Pada tanda disamping berarti?

Kata "beside" memiliki arti yang sama seperti "next to" yang berarti disamping


Contoh kalimat: I stand next to him


Kata beside tergolong dalam prepositions of place



PREPOSITIONS LAINNYA


Between


Mengartikan diantara 2 orang. Apabila diantara lebih dari 2 orang, maka prepositions yang tepat adalah "in the middle of ..."


Contoh: He sit between Judy and Rudy.



Behind


Mengartikan dibelakang


Contoh: the library is behind the class.



On, above, over


Ketiganya memiliki arti yang sama, namun penggunaan yang berbeda. Arti ketiga prepositions tersebut adalah diatas.


Contoh

1. The lamp is over the classroom

2. The pencil is on the table

3. The baloon is above the ground




Sign berarti tanda



Means berarti berarti

34. "beside" is used to?


beside digunakan untuk menyatakan disntara

Beside is used to tell about the location of something. Generally, beside is a preposition which has the same meaning with at the side of or next to. The preposition which is using the word beside, is between two nouns located side by side


Pembahasan

How to use “beside” is a sentence?

For example:

My mom and dad sit beside each other in the dining room.The second bathroom is beside my bedroom.I always put the phone beside my bed every night.
